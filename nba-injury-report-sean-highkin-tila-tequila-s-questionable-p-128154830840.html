NBA Injury Report: Sean Highkin, Tila Tequila’s Questionable Politics, and Grave Robbing

September 1st, 2015 10:26pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

This week Sean Highkin, NBA writer for NBC and lead Bulls writer for Bleacher Report, makes his triumphant return to the show. Long time fans of the podcast will recall that Sean was our FIRST EVER GUEST and it was a delight to have him back. We talk Guns N’ Roses, the CBA, Porzingis’ ceiling, R-rated movies, the month of August and MORE!

On The Rest of the Show (starting at 1:02.40) we talk Slayer, The Sugarcubes, grave robbing/digging, mountain re-naming and so much more. Join us!
