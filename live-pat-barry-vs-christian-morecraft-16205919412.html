Live: Pat Barry vs Christian Morecraft

January 20th, 2012 10:14pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

by Dr. Octagon, J.D.

Pat Barry is a fan favorite.  Hope he finishes this fight.

Round 1-Barry with the right leg kick and gets taken down. Morecraft goes for the choke, but he’s a bum on the ground and Barry is able to stand up. Barry with a nice jab to the face. Barry with another leg kick and Morecraft shoots ineffectively.

Barry is winning the standup so far. Morecraft shoots and gets a takedown again. They scramble, Morecraft goes for the armbar, but Barry gets out of it. I’m no expert on BJJ, but these submissions look sloppy.

Barry goes for the headkick, but it’s blocked. Barry slips a punch and lands a big left hook. Morecraft goes down with a thud, Barry follows him to the ground to finish it, ref steps in. Morecraft was out the moment the left hook landed. Pat HD Barry wins by knockout. He needed that win.
