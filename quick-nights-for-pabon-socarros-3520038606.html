Quick Nights for Pabon, Socarros

February 26th, 2011 4:12am by Stiff Jab Tumblr

(Photos via www.fightnews.com)

Puerto Rican junior welterweight Jesus Pabon flattened Americo Santos in the first round on Boxeo Telemundo to score a WBC Latino trinket in Palm Bay, Fla. on Friday.

Can’t deny the Spanish broadcasts do two things much better than their English counterparts: keep the pre-hype fight short and find stunning ring card girls. The community center gym backdrop was a nice touch that reminded us of junior high school

In the co-feature bout local junior middleweight Francisco “Tony” Ginorio floored Dominican Franklin Gonzalez twice to decisively win a four round decision.

Finally, Cuban junior featherweight Hairon Socarros laid out Puerto Rican Carlos Bruno with a scintillating left hook in the first round. Both fighters were making their professional debuts.
