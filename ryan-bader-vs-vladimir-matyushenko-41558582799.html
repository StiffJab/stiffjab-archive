Ryan Bader vs. Vladimir Matyushenko

January 26th, 2013 6:15pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

Round 1- Bader lands a jab and huge left hook and grabs the neck.  Trying for an arm choke from the bottom, but probably doesn’t have enough leverage.  Gets his legs wrapped for more leverage and Vlad taps.


That lunging hook he threw is the same one that Lyoto Machida knocked him out on with a counterpunch.  You can totally get away with that against Vlad though.

Bader by submission 50 seconds into Round 1. Fastest submission in light heavyweight history.
