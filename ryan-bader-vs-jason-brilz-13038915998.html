Ryan Bader vs Jason Brilz

November 19th, 2011 8:36pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

This is a bounceback fight for Ryan Bader after he was destroyed by Jon Jones and then shocked by Tito Ortiz in the first round. Jason Brilz has also lost his last two fights to Vladimir “The Janitor” Matyushenko and Small Nog, though the loss to Small Nog was controversial.  Bader will probably win this one though.

Round 1-Ryan Bader with straight left and then a right hand behind the ear that levels Brilz. Looked a lot like the punch that knocked out Cain the other night.

Hopefully they’ll try to squeeze in one more fight before the pay per view. Glad to see Darth Bader back.
