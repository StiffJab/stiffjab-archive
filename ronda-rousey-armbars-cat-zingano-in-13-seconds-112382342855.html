Ronda Rousey Armbars Cat Zingano in 13 Seconds

March 1st, 2015 12:03am by Stiff Jab Tumblr

That last fight stunk. Hopefully this fight will be better.

Round 1: Cat Zingano flies at Ronda Rousey and clinches with her, but Ronda does some sort of roll, flips Cat over gets her back, traps her arm and armbars her immediately. Cat taps and it’s over. Ronda wins by armbar in 13 seconds.

That was fucking stupid.  The division is officially cleaned out. No viable competitors left for Rousey. Bring on Cris Cyborg.  

I guess it was actually 14 seconds officially. That was the fastest UFC championship fight ever.

Ronda said she’d like to fight Holly Holm. No shit. Ronda could walk through a few weakass punches and submit her in under a minute.

Cat Zingano says and I quote: “I want to do it again, and just.  .  .  fuck.” That’s an awkward interview.

Bethe Correia is probably the most viable competitor for Ronda currently in the UFC and she hasn’t fought anyone good. It’s Cyborg or squash matches.
