Live: Magomed Saadulaev vs Dave Jansen

October 19th, 2012 10:40pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

by Dr. Octagon, J.D.

Round 1: Both fighters open by kicking. Dave Jansen is stalking Magomed Saadulaev. Magomed shoots and gets the takedown, but the Russian is on top. Jansen is able to get up and is now on top. The Russian stands and tries for a kimura, but Jansen gets up. Magomed trying for a standing guillotine and Dave’s face is pretty red. Magomed gets a body triangle locked in and has both underhooks in the transition. Jansen rolls out and tries to stand up but Magomed controls him.  [[MORE]]

Magomed tries for an armbar or something; I don’t know what that was supposed to be but Jansen ends up on top. Magomed gets up and lands a left. Another left from Magomed. The Russian shoots and gets the takedown again, but Jansen goes for a guillotine. Magomed gets his head out and Jansen gets back to the feet as the round ends.

10-9 Saadulaev.

Round 2: The Russian swings, and grabs a single leg. Jansen gets his head out. Jansen lands a jab. Magomed shoots and lands a punch as the fighters disengage. Jansen lands an uppercut. Magomed looks tired. Jansen lands a jab. Magomed lands a right and an uppercut. Magomed lands a kick and then a left. Magomed lands three more lefts. Jansen shoots and gets the takedown. Magomed is going for an arm.

Jansen is going for an arm triangle from inside the guard, which isn’t going to work. Probably just gassed his arms there. Jansen postures up. Now he’s back in the guard and working the ground and pound. Lots of shots from the top position.

Round 3: Magomed gets a body lock. Jansen pulls guard into the guillotine and gets the tap. Magomed taps and passes out seeming at the same time, going totally limp.

Dave Jansen by submission at 41 seconds of Round 3
