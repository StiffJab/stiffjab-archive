Matt Brown vs Mike Swick

December 8th, 2012 8:31pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

by Dr. Octagon, J.D.

Round 1:  Mike Swick tags Matt Brown as he comes in. Matt Brown throws a left and then grabs a single leg and gets the takedown. Matt Brown had a D'arce choke locked in with a body triangle. Brown lets go and Swick is now in his guard. Brown looking for a triangle, but doesn’t get it. Swick is in the guard and now Brown has a triangle locked in. Swick grabs on to Matt Brown and these two guys are completely tied up together. Swick gets out of it and now has a guillotine, but lets it go and they’re back up. Matt Brown is throwing bombs against the cage.

10-9 Matt Brown.

Round 2: Brown lands a left hand. Brown just misses a head kick. Brown with a nice right elbow. Swick has Brown pushed  up against the fence, but the fighters break. Swick locks in the thai clinch and lands some knees. Matt Brown lands another elbow. Matt Brown lands a left right combo that puts Swick on his ass; Swick is totally out.

Matt Brown by KO.
