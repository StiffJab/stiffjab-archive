Bellator Live: Mike Pyle vs Josh Neer

June 8th, 2012 9:50pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

by Dr. Octagon, J.D.

Chris Adams, the second worst ref in MMA after Kim Winslow, is officiating. Mike Pyle has a mullet going on that was almost certainly crafted with a vintage Flowbee. Ghastly.

Round 1: Pyle throws and gets a takedown. Josh Neer is able to get a leg lock, but Pyle is out of it and on top again. Neer almost lands in an armbar, but Pyle slides out of it and is back in the closed guard. Pyle landed another nice one as Neer moved the guard up too high. Neer gets a leg, but Pyle gets out of it. Pyle looks hurt.

Neer is working Mike Pyle against the cage. Pyle lands two big knees. Neer with a nice pair of bodyshots and a knee to the face. Pyle is just covering up, I think that cut on his left eye is hurting him badly. Pyle lands a glancing left, and Neer walks face first into a short right directly on the jawbone. Neer crumples to the canvas. Mike Pyle by KO at 4:56 in the First Round.
