Igor Pokrajac v. Todd Brown

March 3rd, 2011 9:31pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

Pokravac is a Cro Cop protege so expect sharp striking.

Round 1- Pokravac is teeing off on Brown, who looks flabby and out of his element. They tie up on the cage, but Brown doesn’t know what he’s doing here either. Just sort of holding him against the cage.

Igor lands a nice knee to the head. The ref should really have pulled them apart about 30 seconds ago. Todd Brown landing weak knees.They break up and Igor goes back to attacking, but gets caught with a hook.  Brown again pushes Igor against the cage and looks like his fat ass is catching his breath again. They break up again. 

Brown is bleeding and getting lit up again. Hard right hook to the temple and Brown goes down, igor gets on top of him, but doesn’t do any damage.

They stand up, huge knee by Igor in the Thai clinch knocks Brown down.  Punches Todd in the side of the head maybe ten times. He can’t get up and says he is done after the round ends. 

Igor says he wants Tito.  Who wouldn’t want to fight Tito?
