Chan Sung Jung KOs Hominick in :07

December 10th, 2011 9:30pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

This is a huge upset. Hominick is a top five featherweight. Chan Sung Jung was not considered to be among the elite. His last two fights he’s won with the first ever twister in UFC history and then he ties the UFC knockout record held by Todd Duffee. Love watching this guy fight.
