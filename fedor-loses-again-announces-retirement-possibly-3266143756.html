Fedor Loses Again, Announces Retirement (possibly)

February 13th, 2011 12:03am by Stiff Jab Tumblr

Fedor loses again to a much larger opponent.  He has had a great career and it was amazing that he was able to survive the second round without being submitted, but it might be time for him to retire.  He says that this might be the last time that he fights and it is God’s will.

In other news Dana White has just tweeted a smiley face emoticon.  I am sure that he could not be happier with how terribly this fight has gone for Strikeforce.  Three terrible fights and their most marketable fighter loses and announces his retirement.  They will be lucky if they can afford to finish the Grand Prix.
