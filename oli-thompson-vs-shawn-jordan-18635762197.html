Oli Thompson vs. Shawn Jordan

March 2nd, 2012 8:35pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

Round 1- Jordan lands a nice left.  Oli Thompson lands a combination.  Jordan lands again with the uppercut, but Oli’s lush beard cushioned the blow.  Jordan si landing a lot of punches.  Thompson returns with some shots of his own.  Not a lot of defense in this bout.  Oli lands a jab.  Shawn lands a nice uppercut  a knee a left hand and is swarming Oli on the ground, but Oli stands and gets in a nice kimura attempt, but Jordan rolls out of it and the fighters go to the feet.  Jordan lands a big knee.  Nice bodyshot by Jordan.  Oli is bleeding.  Oli lands a knee as they disengage.  Jordan throws a jab.  Oli throws a superman punch and Jordan swarms Oli against the cage, but Olis covers up and fires back with some shots of his own.  Oli sticks out his tongue and the fighters touch gloves in acknowledgment of the mutual asswhooping they just put on each other.  Really great first round.

Round 2- Jordan walks face first into a right.  Fighters exchange blows.  I think Jordan is landing the cleaner shots.  Ref tells Thompson not to head butt, Thompson looks baffled.  Fighters look tired.  Jordan landsa nother jab.  Oli is cut on his eye and on his head.  Oli throws a superman punch, doesn’t land.  Jordan lands a pair of punches.  Oli shoots and it is stuffed.  Jordan lands another jab.  Jordan landing punches in flurries now.  Oli’s grillpiece looks like shit.  He’s eating jabs nonstop.  Jordan lands another upppercut to the beard and Oli looks horribly hurt.  I think he is out on his feet.  Jordan is cautious and probably tired from the vast amount of punches he has been throwing.  I don’t think he knows how hurt Oli is.  Don’t know how he is standing.  Jordan keeps pouring on the punches and Oli really isn’t defending.  Thompson is getting destroyed as he goes to the ground.    Thank god, the ref stops it.  Backflip from Shawn Jordan.  Athletic heavyweight.  Looked explosive, but he needs to work on his defense.

Shawn Jordan by TKO - Round 2 1:07
