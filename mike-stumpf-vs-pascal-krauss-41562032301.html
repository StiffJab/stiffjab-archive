Mike Stumpf vs Pascal Krauss

January 26th, 2013 7:01pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

by Dr. Octagon, J.D.

Round 1: Pascal Krauss lands a jab. Mike Stumpf with a leg kick.  Pair of punches for Stumpf.  Leg kick by Stumpf.  Krauss lands a right and a jab.  Stumpf lands a jab and Krauss lands an uppercut.  Right by Stumpf.  Leg kick by Krauss.  Krauss lands a pair of punches and a leg kick and gets taken down by Stumpf.  Krauss pushes Stumpf off and the fighters stand.  Uppercut by Krauss.  Fighters  linch and both fighters land.  Jab by Krauss.  Krauss lands a jab and a leg kick.  Krass’ striking looks really crisp.  Krauss lands knees from the clinch and Stumpf lands a punch.  Leg kikc by Stumpf.  Leg kick by Krauss.  Krauss is pumping the jab.  Nice front kick by Krauss to the face.  Leg kick by Krauss.  Big uppercut by Krauss and Stumpf is hurt, but gets a hold of a single leg and works for a takedown. He doesn’t get it and the round ends.

10-9 Krauss.[[MORE]]

Round 2- Jab by Krauss.  Leg kick by Krauss.  Uppercut by Krauss.  Font kick by Krauss.   Stumpf is getting pick apart on the feet.  Just got hit with an uppercut and an elbow.  Krauss is landing at will.  Jab.  right hook.  right hook.  Left.   Leg kick.  Superman uppercut from Krauss.  Straight right from Krauss.  Stumpf is game and is coming forward, but doesn’t have the technicque to hang.  Needs a homerun.  Uppercut leg kick from Krauss.  Krauss shoots for some reason.  Stumpf outwrestles Krauss and is on top of Krauss, who is trying to sit up against the cage.  Stumpf isn’t doing shit and the ref should stand them up.  Krauss is landing some bodyshots from the bottom.  North south position and Krauss stands up.  Stumpf lands a knee from the clinch, but is now being pushed against the cage and Krauss is working knees and shots in from close.  Stumpf lands some big punches as the fighters break.  Stumpf goes for a takedown again and ends up in a headlock against the cage.  Krauss is working the knees against the cage.  Nasty knees to the face.  Stumpf is bleeding badly from the nose.  Stumpf lands a leg kick as the round ends.

Round 3- Stumpf needs a miracle.  Krauss lands a few punches.  Stumpf still coming forward, but Krauss backs him up against the cage and lands another uppercut and Stumpf loses the mouthpiece and they stop for a second.  Krauss lands another superman uppercut.  Krauss swarming and trying to end the fight, but Stumpf is still on his feet.  Fighters clinch and Krauss lands a big knee from the clinch and then wisely steps back where he ahs the advantage.  Krauss lands a body kick and a right.  Another right and another leg kick.  Stumpf is really tough, but not much else.  Stumpf shoots and Krauss stuffs it.  Anohter uppercut by Krauss.  Stumpf shoots and Krauss stuffs it and takes him down and is in the full guard.  Krauss has some great cardio as he’s really working from the top position.

Judges score it 30-27 unanimously for Krauss.
