Sean Jordan vs Mike Russow

January 26th, 2013 6:03pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

by Dr. Octagon, J.D.

Mike Russow is the hometown hero for knocking out the massive, TRT-enhanced Todd Duffee in the third round with a broken arm.  Spent the day waiting for Verizon to come.  They never showed.  Paid for a phone line and they said that they didn’t need to come.  Turned out not to be true.  They don’t even give you a window anymore.  Just the whole fucking day. Live updates after the jump:[[MORE]]

Round 1- Russow pushes Jordan against the cage.  Fighters break.  Russow lands a big right and Jordan is practically out.  Russow lands another pair of punches and a knee, but Jordan gets his wits about him and returns fire.  Russow shoots for the single, but lets it go.  Jordan is bleeding badly from above his left eye and below his right eye.  Pair of rights from Mike Russow.  Knee by Jordan from the clinch.  Russow is working the body.  Fighters break again.  Russow lands a right.  Jordan’s defense isn’t great.  Eats another right.  And another.  Russow shoves Jordan against the cage and pops him a few times.  Jordan keeping his hand down so he can’t be kneed.  Russow lands the right again.  Jordan comes forward with haymakers, but misses.  Another right by Russow.  Russow grabs a leg, but Jordan rolls out of it.  Jordan keeping the hand down again to avoid knees.  Jordan swinging for the fences, but Russow pushes him against the cage again.

Round 2- Jordan landed a right and an uppercut.  Russow got poked in the eye, which is funny because Dana just said on twitter how seldom they occur.  Right hand by Russo, but eats a bunch of punches from Jordan.  Russow landed another right, but Jordan is coming forward and takes Russow down.  Jordan has a full mount with four minutes left to work.  Jordan is landing big punches.  Jordan gets on the back and is punches him from behind.  Russow goes for a footlock, and is able to stand as he lets it go.  The graphics are falling off of Russow’s shorts.  Russow lands a knee to the head from the clinch.  Jordan grabs a leg and again is on top with two minutes left to work.  Ground and pound from the full mount.  Russow gives up his back and Jordan is teeing off as Herb Dean stops it.

Sean Jordan by TKO 3:38 of Round 2.
