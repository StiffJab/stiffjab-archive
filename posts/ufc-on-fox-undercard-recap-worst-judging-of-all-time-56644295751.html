UFC on Fox Undercard Recap: Worst Judging of All Time

July 27th, 2013 8:07pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

by Dr. Octagon, J.D.

Michael Chiesa vs. Jorge Masvidal: Chiesa looked great in the first round and was even outstriking Masvidal at the start. In the second, the fight went to the ground, where Masvidal forced Chiesa to tap via a D'arce choke just before the horn.

Danny Castillo vs. Tim Means: Boring fight. Castillo won a 29-28 unanimous decision.

Mac Danzig vs. Melvin Guillard: As expected, Melvin Guillard’s power was too much for Mac Danzig. Guillard landed a huge left and finished it with hammerfists in the second.

Daron Cruickshank vs. Yves Edwards: Snoozer of a fight. I scored it 29-28 for Edwards. Two judges scored it 30-27 for Cruickshank, the other had it 30-27 for Edwards. The judges are all over the place tonight. Mighty Mouse should consider finishing a fight for once.

Ed Herman vs. Trevor Smith: Sick fight. Nothing but action. Two judges saw the fight completely differently again, one giving it to Herman 30-27, one giving it to Smith 30-27, and one giving it to Herman 29-28. They’ll get a nice check from that fight either way.

Germaine De Randamie vs. Julie Kedzie: De Randamie pretty much smoked the noticeably smaller Kedzie for the whole fight. Kedzie got two takedowns, but didn’t do shit with them. One judge actually gave Kedzie the fight 29-28, but the other two saw it for De Randamie, one 30-27 and the other 29-28.

DOC OCTAGON UNDERDOG SIREN: TONIGHT’S JUDGES ARE BLIND. BIG-TIME RISK FOR THE CHAMP, WHO LIVES BY THE DECISION.
