Stipe Miocic vs Stefan Struve

September 29th, 2012 6:57pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

by Dr. Octagon, J.D. 

Croat-American versus a Dutchman here. Stipe MIocic is a great athlete, Stefan Struve is really tall and has a ton of fights (29) given his tender age (24). The announcers just said Struve is still growing and is now listed at 7 feet tall?!?!?! Struve can finish a fight on the feet or with a submission, but doesn’t have the same raw athleticism and strength that some of the other heavyweights have. Live updates after the jump:[[MORE]]

Round 1- Stipe throwing punches.  Struve kicks.  Stipe throws a superman punch.  Struve lands a jab.  Stipe with a shot to the body.  Struve head kick is blocked.  Stipe landed a right.  Stipe to the body.  Struve with leg kicks.  Stipe throws a left.  Struve needs to pump the jab more.  Stipe is getting in on him way too easily.  Stipe landed a left and then an overhand right.  Head kick landed by Struve.  Stipe with a pair of punches to the body.  Struve throws the knee, but it misses.  Stipe lands a right.  Another right.  Leg kick from Stipe.  Struve goes with the knee, but Stipe catches it.  Stipe lets him stand.  Shin to the face of Stipe.  Miocic lands a punch, looks like he rattles Struve a little bit with that.  Struve throws a knee.  Stipe is jabbing.  Stipe lands a left.  Stipe catches the leg, but doesn’t land the punch he throws in return.  Stipe with two shots to the body.  Knee from Struve.  Struve with a big leg kick.  Left to the face lands for Stipe.  Stipe landsa  jab.  Stipe lands another punch.

10-9 Miocic.  Don’t buy Hewlett Packard laptops.  This thing is so hot that I wouldn’t be surprised if I’ve been rendered sterile.

Round 2- Ripping body kick from Struve.  Good god man.  Struve with a shot to the cock and apologizes.  Struve lands a kick to the body.  Stipe lands a pair of punches, then another pair of punches.  Stipe is looking good in the striking.  Stefan lands a jab.  Stipe lands a hook.  Finally Struve is snapping the jab, the official punch of this weblog.  Struve landed a nice left to Stipe’s right eye.  Struve is has Miocic who is hurt and backing up.  Seems to get his wits about him and thorws a really slow uppercut.  Struve lands another uppercut and two punches.  Miocic is winded and concussed I think.  Miocic is coming fowrard, but I think he wiffed.  Miocic really caught struve with a right hand.  Struve wobbled.  Miocic lands an uppercut.  Miocic landed two punches.  Struve has forgottten about the jab again. Struve lands an enormous pair of punches and follows Stipe landing another pair of power punches.  Stipe is hurt.  Miocic slips and Strve throws a right and knocks him out on his feet.  Struve continues to throw punches, all right hands, and Stipe’s arms aren’t up at all now, but he is still standing in Zombie mode.  Herb Dean steps in and stops the fight after struve lands a few more uppercuts.

Struve wins by KO Round 2- 3:50
