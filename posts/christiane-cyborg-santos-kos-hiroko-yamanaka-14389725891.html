Christiane Cyborg Santos KOs Hiroko Yamanaka

December 18th, 2011 12:03am by Stiff Jab Tumblr

by Dr. Octagon, J.D.

Quick night for the seemingly invincible Cyborg. She lands a monster right, the ref looks to step in and Cyborg stops, but the ref then lets them continue. Cyborg swarms and nails Yamanaka again, forcing the ref to stop it.  It might have been a little bit premature, but who cares. Yamanaka was going to get killed no matter what.
