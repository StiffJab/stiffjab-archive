T.J. Dillashaw vs Vaughan Lee

July 11th, 2012 8:53pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

by Dr. Octagon, J.D.

Bantamweight fight here. 135 pounds bra. Vaughan Lee does his emo heart hand symbol and Mrs. Octagon mocks him heartily. He is a very sensitive Englishman.

Round 1: First minute passes without any action. Then Vaughan Lee starts to pour on the punishment. Vaughan Lee throwing bombs, but I’m not sure they are landing. T.J. Dillashaw shoots, but Lee gets out of the way. TJ swinging, but Vaughan is able to get out of the way again. Vaughan throws a big kick and Dillashaw has the takedown.

Vaughan gets up, but T.J. is on him. You know these Englishmen can’t wrestle bra. Dillashaw drags him back to the ground and locks in the body triangle. He’s on Lee’s back while Lee is standing. Lee is not defending this well. Dillashaw has it up too high and isn’t under the chin, but he’s got the choke tight over the jaw. Vaughan Lee taps out. 

T.J. Dillashaw wins by tapout due to rear naked choke at 2:33 in the first.
