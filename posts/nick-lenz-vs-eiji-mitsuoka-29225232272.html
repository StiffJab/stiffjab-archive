Nick Lenz vs Eiji Mitsuoka

August 11th, 2012 7:40pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

by Dr. Octagon, J.D.

Nick Lenz came through with an absolutely dominant victory over Eiji Mitsuoka, popping him on the feet, scoring on 4 of 4 takedowns and finally winning by stoppage near the end of Round 1 viarepeated blows to the Mitsuoka’s turtled head. Lenz looks very strong as a lightweight and said he feels much stronger at this weight. Mitsuoka was never in the fight.

Lenz by TKO and that is it for the Facebook prelims. The rest of the prelims will be on FX.
