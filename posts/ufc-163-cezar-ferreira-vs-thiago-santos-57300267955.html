UFC 163: Cezar Ferreira vs Thiago Santos

August 3rd, 2013 11:21pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

by Dr. Octagon, J.D.

Joe Rogan, Arianny and the Britney all sat this one out. Goldberg and Bruce Buffer are here though.

Cezar Ferreira is from Vitor Belfort’s camp and looks pretty muscular.

Round 1: Left hook by Ferreira. Thiago Santos is wobbled. Massive, Belfortian left kick to the head by Ferreira. Wow! That looked like the kick that Vitor used to put out DJ Mikie B.

Ferreira locks in the guillotine and Santos taps immediately. Ferreira jumps out of the cage, but they tell him to go back and talk to Brian Stann.
