Strikeforce Interlude: Interviews

February 12th, 2011 10:52pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

The small shouty Spanish guy is interviewing Overeem and Werdum, asking Overeem why he called out Werdum. He doesn’t give the real reason, that he’s a roided-up striker and can just stand back and throw bombs.

Werdum looks like his nose was freshly-broken seems pretty pumped that he beat Overeem back in the day, but that was forty pounds ago.  Overeem thinks there is a 60/40 chance of Fedor winning.

Some girl is interviewing Barnett and Brett Rogers and asking if it is a disadvantage that he hasn’t fought most of these dudes.  Josh Barnett says that he is sitting on a throne full of skulls or something like that.  Brett thinks that “Karkov” is going to win the fight.  Thought the announcers mangled his name as badly as could be done, but Brett outdid them handily.
