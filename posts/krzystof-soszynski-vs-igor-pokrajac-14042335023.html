Krzystof Soszynski vs Igor Pokrajac

December 10th, 2011 8:39pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

by Dr. Octagon, J.D.

Battle of the Eastern Europeans here.

Round 1-Fighters exchange and it looks like Igor hurt Krzystof with a right. Pokrajac swarms the Pole lands a few more shots on the ground, looks like the Polish Experiment still thinks he is back in Warsaw. Sick knockout by Pokrajac. 

Not a lot of decisions on the undercard. Lots of good fights so far.
