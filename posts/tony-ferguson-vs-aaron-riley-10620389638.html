Tony Ferguson vs Aaron Riley

September 24th, 2011 8:45pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

Tony “Turd” Ferguson has a massive reach advantage. I hate Tony Ferguson and his stupid angel wing tattoos, but he looks pretty ripped. Wish Bruce Buffer would take some cues from his much better-dressed brother.

Round 1-Ferguson hits him with a nice left uppercut and Riley is hurt really badly. He landed a few more after the initial shot. That jaw is getting wired shut after the fight.  Ferguson is following Riley around the cage and throwing bombs. Riley can’t close his mouth, which is pouring blood.

Riley tells his corner that his jaw is broken. No shit. The doctors are stopping this one.  Ferguson wins. Amazing that he finished the round. That jaw looks awful.
