New York MMA Legalization Update

December 24th, 2010 2:45am by Stiff Jab Tumblr

Fighters Only reports one of the reasons pro-MMA legislation is being held up in New York (in addition to the sclerotic legislature and incompetent governor) is opposition created by a hotel labor union in order to get back at the Fertitta brothers for anti-union activity in their Las Vegas casino chain.
