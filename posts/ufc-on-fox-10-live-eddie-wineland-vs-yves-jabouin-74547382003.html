UFC on Fox 10 Live: Eddie Wineland vs Yves Jabouin

January 25th, 2014 7:27pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

by Dr. Octagon, J.D.

Round 1: Eddie Wineland has rad tats. Wineland starts the first round by coming forward, then lands a leg kick. Yves Jabouin lands a left. Yves throws a kick. Eddie blocks it. Yves lands a left, Eddie responds in kind. Eddie blocks another kick. Wineland with a shot to the body. More live updates after the jump:[[MORE]]

Eddie lands a left. Yves throws another kick and Eddie blocks it. Yves comes in and Eddie lands a right. Head kick by Yves was blocked. Another kick blocked. Eddie lands a counter right. Eddie lands a jab. Wineland lands a right to the body and a left to the head. Eddie lands a big right.

Eddie caught with a kick to the face, but keeps coming forward. Big leg kick from Eddie. Kick from Yves. Yves throws a ton of kicks to the body. Yves lands a right. Eddie shoots. Wineland gets two underhooks,, then lands knees to the body.

10-9 Yves Jabouin.

Round 2: Wineland chasing, eats a left. A right lands for Eddie. Eddie has a cut on his head. Yves lands a right, then a left. Eddie with a right to the body. Yves has his kick blocked. A pair of punches land for Eddie. That got Yves’ attention. Spinning back first by Yves misses. Right by Eddie lands. Pair of punches by Eddie land. Yves lands a spinning back kick.

Eddie lands a few punches, and Yves takes notice. Yves lands a head kick, but Eddie walks through it. Eddie lands a big right hand, flooring Yves and climbs on top. Wineland has a full mount and locks in a nice arm triangle, but he lets it go and starts punishing Yves with elbows from the top position.

Yves gives up his back and Eddie lands tons of punches on the turtled Yves. The ref finally steps in.

Eddie Wineland by TKO.
