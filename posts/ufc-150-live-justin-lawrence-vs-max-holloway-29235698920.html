UFC 150 Live: Justin Lawrence vs Max Holloway

August 11th, 2012 10:31pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

by Dr. Octagon, J.D.

Round 1: Max Holloway kicks and misses. Justin Lawrence lands a left. Lawrence lands the overhand right. Lawrence with a left. Lawrence lands a few punches there. Lawrence with a left hook. Holloway with a shot to Lawrence’s penis. Ouch. Lawrence is cut a little bit. Holloway kicks. Lawrence shoots and gets the takedown, but Holloway works back up to the feet.

Looks like Lawrence got caught on the way in there and is bleeding a ton. Lawrence lands two left jabs and then a nice right. Two nice punches from Lawrence. Lawrence with a hook to the body. Nice combination from Lawrence. Lawrence lands a left hook. Another shot to the penis from Holloway as the round ends.

10-9 Lawrence.

Round 2: Right by Lawrence. Kick by Lawrence. Max Holloway with a head kick.  Leg kick from Lawrence. The fighters exchange. Spinning backfist from Lawrence,  didn’t do shit. Holloway with a nice two-punch combination. Lawrence with a leg kick. Lawrence with an overhand right. Lawrence with another right. Jab by Lawrence. Lawrence shoots, but is stuffed.

Uppercut by Lawrence. Lawrence with a kick to the body. Two-punch combination by Lawrence. Lawrence with left. Lawrence shoots and gets stuffed. Lawrence uncorks a few shots, but doesn’t land much. Nice knee by Holloway with a counter punch by Lawrence. Holloway lands a vicious left bodyshot and Lawrence is hurt badly. Holloway lands a lot of shots and referee Rosenthal stops it at 4:49 in Round 2.

Holloway by TKO

Seemed like everyone was picking Lawrence to win that one. It was a pretty competitive fight right up until the end.
