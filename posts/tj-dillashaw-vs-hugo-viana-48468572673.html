TJ Dillashaw vs. Hugo Viana

April 20th, 2013 5:25pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

Round 1- Dillashaw comes in and gets hit with a jab.  TJ comes in and gets hit with what looks like a huge left hook and pops right back up and grabs a single gets a takedown via a single leg, takes the back and looks for the Team Alpha Male classic rear naked choke, but Viana powers out of it and is back to his feet.  Viana landsa a glancing elbow while TJ has him pushed against the cage.  TJ has a single leg and is working hard for the takedown, but Viana stays up.  TJ’s nose is cut.  Hugo pumping the jab.  Viana eats a left jab coming in and goes down, but pops back up.  TJ lunging, misses the left and lands a right hook.  Viana misses a kick.  TJ whiffs on a high kick.  TJ with a front kick, which also misses.  TJ lands a glancing right, shots the single and lets it go.  TJ thows a body punch.  TJ apparently doesn’t fear the takedown, keeps throwing high kicks.  TJ lands a right that drops Viana, Viana looks hurt, but is back up and able to escape.  Dillashaw going in in for the kill pops Viana a few times on the feet, finishes it on the ground.

Hugo Viana actually looked pretty good in that fight before the end, but TJ Dillashaw was too much for him.

Dillashaw by TKO 4:22 in the first
