UFC 150 Live: Jared Hamman vs Michael Kuiper

August 11th, 2012 9:08pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

by Dr. Octagon, J.D.

Round 1: Leg kicks from Jared Hamman. Hamman with shots to the body, both punches and kicks. Nice leg kick by Hamman. Michael Kuiper is landing the crisper punches though. Kuiper gets a body lock and works the takedown, but Hamman is able to work back up to the feet.

Kuiper lands a right, but Hamman is the more active fighter. Kuiper lands a leg kick and Hamman is hurt. Kuiper lands a big right, comes forward and is landing shots. Hamman is hurt, but is still on his feet. Kuiper unloads on him, but Hamman is able to keep his wits about him. Kuiper uncorks another shot and Hamman looks mildly concussed. Hamman keeps on throwing weak punches, but Kuiper just keeps on blocking them, coming forward and unloading. Hamman is taking a ton of punishment and it looks like his leg is hurt from that kick.

Round 2: Kuiper with another leg kick and Hamman looks really hurt. Hamman lands a right. Kuiper with a left.  Kuiper is ripping the leg kicks. Hamman lands a bunch of punches. Kuiper uncorks a big right. I can’t believe Hamman is on his feet.

One-two from Kuiper and Hamman is out. Kuiper is landing shots to his head. Hamman is apparently still awake and works to his feet. Jesus. He’s taking a lot of punishment.

Kuiper lands another massive right; Hamman is out on his feet and wobbling around the ring. Kuiper lands one more left and the ref calls it. Hamman took a vicious beating before he went down.

Michael Kuiper by TKO at 2:16 in Round 2
