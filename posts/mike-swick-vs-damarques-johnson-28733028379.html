Mike Swick vs Damarques Johnson

August 4th, 2012 9:19pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

by Dr. Octagon, J.D.

Round 1: Mike Swick lands a huge right and ties up Damarques Johnson with double underhooks. Swick again with a right, this isn’t going to go too well for Johnson if he keeps eating those. Another right by Swick. Damarques is bleeding from his mouth again. Johnson gets hit again and takes a knee from the clinch, but Swick slips and Damarques is on top of him; Johnson gets a nice elbow off.

Swick is against the cage and is trying to sit up, but Damarques is keeping him down. Swick is up and tees off on Damarques, who is not defending the right hand still. Swick landing knees from the clinch, but Damarques trips him and gets his back. Damarques has him mounted and is going for a neck crank, but lets it go. Damarques is popping Swick from the mount. He’s landing a lot of shots.

Damarques goes for another choke and Swick rolls out. Now Damarques is on the side looking for a D'arce choke with about 45 seconds left. Swick pops out of it and now Damarques is in his guard.

Round 2: Swick’s face is fucked. Swick lands a right. Swick with the left hook. Damarques kicks, Swick catches the leg and puts Damarques down on the ground. Swick follows it up with a punch on the ground that knocks Damarques out.

Mike Swick by KO at 1:20 in the Second
