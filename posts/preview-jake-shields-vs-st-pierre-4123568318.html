Preview: Jake Shields vs. St. Pierre

March 26th, 2011 10:41pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

Jake says he is ready to go. He’s been training with Cort McGee and Chael Sonnen (who is now back in action after almost beating Anderson Silva, testing positive for steroids, claiming that it was due to low testosterone and then pleading guilty to tax fraud).

Shields didn’t look too impressive in his last fight, but it was his first fight as a welterweight. Everyone says that he is freakishly strong and he beat Dan Henderson at middleweight. Jake has great submissions, and will hopefully give St. Pierre more of a challenge than Koschek did.
