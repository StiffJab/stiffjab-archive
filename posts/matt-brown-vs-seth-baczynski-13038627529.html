Matt Brown vs Seth Baczynski

November 19th, 2011 8:29pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

by Dr. Octagon, J.D.

Round 1-Seth Baczynski lands the first good shot with a right hand. Seth pushes Brown up against the fence, but Brown is slicker in the dirty boxing department. Brown gives much better than he gets despite the fact that his back is up against the fence for most of the exchange.

Fighters break up, Seth gets the takedown and lands a slow elbow. Brown’s guard is closed and Seth is trying to get out of it. Brown sweeps and Seth is on his back. Brown lets him stand. Brown lands a nice body shot before Seth pins him against the fence again. Brown lands another right knee to the stomach. Seth goes for a standing guillotine as the round ends.

Good round for both fighters. Brown might have done more damage, but Seth is going to win the round on the cards for being the aggressor and getting the takedown.

Round 2-Brown gets the takedown, but falls into a tight guillotine choke. Brown is really working to get his head out, wow, defending it properly and working his ass off, but Seth is a strong guy and is not letting it out. Matt Brown taps. 

Seth Baczynski wins due to tapout by guillotine choke
