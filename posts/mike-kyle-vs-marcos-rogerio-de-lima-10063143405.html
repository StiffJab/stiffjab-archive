Mike Kyle vs Marcos Rogerio de Lima

September 10th, 2011 10:17pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

Round 1-Not much action so far. Fighters are engaging, unlike the last opening round we saw, but neither has landed anything of note in the first two minutes. Kyle throws a punch and MRdL looked like he was hurt by it, falling backward and leaning against the cage. No idea what was up with that. MRdL looks lost in the ring. Kyle lands another nice shot. de Lima looks like he wants to stop fighting. Kyle 10-9

Round 2-de Lima looks more game this round. He looks to be getting the better of the exchanges. Kyle gets a takedown with one minute to go but de Lima is able to stand up against the cage. Kyle now has his back and gets another takedown.

Kyle stole that one at the end 10-9.

Round 3-Not much going on but both fighters are pretty active. Kyle scores a nice takedown with about two minutes left and lands some shots from top position. De Lima is bleeding pretty bad now. Kyle won this one for sure.
