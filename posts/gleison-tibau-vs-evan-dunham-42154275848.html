Gleison Tibau vs Evan Dunham

February 2nd, 2013 10:05pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

by Dr. Octagon, J.D.

Round 1: Gleison Tibau looks way bigger than Evan Dunham.  Evan comes forward and lands three punches.  Tibau comes forward swinging wildly.  Tibau lands a hook.  Dunham lands a left.  Dunham is way crisper in the striking, but Tibau probably has more pop behind him.  Tibau’s had enough with the striking and shoots and locks in a front choke as Dunham stands and pulls guard.  Has the mount now and the larger Tibau is on top of Dunham who is looking to stand up.  Now in north south position.  Dunham stands up and pushes Tibau against the cage.  Lands a few knees.  Dunham lands a right as they disengage.  Dunham throws a kick.  blocked.  Gleison is missing on a ton of punches.  Dunham lands a right hook.  Dunham shoots and Tibau turns him around.  Dunham lands a nice knee from the clinch.  Dunham landing body shots. [[MORE]]

Round 2- Dunham lands a left.  Dunham lands a right hook.  Gleison shoots, gives up.  Left landed by Dunham.  Left by Dunham.  Pair of punches by Dunham, but Tibau landed a left.  Dunham shoots on the larger Brazilian, who is able to pop up.  Dunham landing knees to the legs. Dunham looking to tire out Gleison via grappling.  Gleison cuts a ton of weight and is really muscular so should tire out faster according to broscience.  Dunham comes forward, but Tibau lands a left  Dunham shoots again, Tibau stuffs it.  Evan lands a jab.  Evan is punching effectively.  Going in and getting out while Tibau swings for the fences.  Dunham lands an uppercut.  Dunham shoots as the round ends.

Round 3- Dunham lands a left.  Tibau shoots .  Dunham loses a guillotine.  Gleison looks for a guillotine, but Dunham pops his head out.  Dunham shoves Tibau against the cage and Dunham lands an elbow.  Dunham is picking tibau apart on the feet.  I think Tibau is whiffing on two thirds of his punches.  Dunham shoots and it is stuffed.  Dunham lands.  Dunham lands again.  Tibau’s punches come out way after Dunham has moved out of range.  Tibau shoots and Dunham stuffs it.  Dunham lands a few more shots.  Tibau’s eye is starting to swell.  Tibau shoots lazily and it is stuffed.  Evan shoots and it is stuffed.  Dunham pushes Tibau against the cage and lands some shots from the clinch.  Nice knee by Dunham and punches tibau a few more times.  dunham can just stand in front of him and swing.  big jab by Dunham.  Dunahm is teeing off with one minute left to go.  Dunham shoots again and Tibau is out of gas, but stuffs it.  Dunham lands another pair of punches.  Tibau lands a right there.  Dunham lands a massive flying knee which rattles Tibau.  Another knee by Dunham.  Tibau has no defense whatsoever.

Judges score it 29-28 Dunham, 29-28 Tibau and 29-28 Dunham

I can’t believe someone gave that second round to Tibau.  Even Tibau’s corner told him that he lost it.  These fucking judges are a bunch of clowns.  time to pull the trigger on the Pay-Per-View
