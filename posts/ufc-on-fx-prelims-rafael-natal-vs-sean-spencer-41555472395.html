UFC on FX Prelims: Rafael Natal vs Sean Spencer

January 26th, 2013 5:33pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

by Dr. Octagon, J.D.

This card is fucking stacked bra.  A really solid free card and the best part is that the undercard is going to be in high definition because it’s on FX.  It drives me crazy when they have the prelims on FuelTV.  The flyweights aren’t so well known as it’s a new weight class that is basically just absorbing all the guys who were small enough to cut down that far.  Lot of good fighters on this card though.  It should be pretty good.  Spencer’s a late replacement and could fight down a weight class with time to cut weight. Live updates after the jump:[[MORE]]

Round 1- Eye poke by Natal, who apologizes.  Doctor comes in to check it out.  Doctor holding up his fingers. Doctor says he’s good to go.  Kick by Natal, Spencer punches.  Spencer lands a nice right.  Natal needs to take this to the ground.  Leg kick by Natal. Spencer staying active with the hands, but Natal is retreating effectively.  Natal clipped Spencer with a right hook, but he looks alright.  Natal lands a leg kick.  Spencer with a left.  Spinning backfist leg kick combination by Natal, but only the leg kick landed.  Spencer ducks under a punch and lands a left hook.  Pair of punches landed by Natal.    Natal lands a pair of punches.  Natal looks to shoot, but S;pencer stuffs it.  Spencer in the clinch and eats a knee.  Fighters break with a minute left to go.  Side kick by Natal.  Spinning backfist by Natal lands.  Natal lands a pair of punches and is in the full mount, but only has twenty seconds to work.  Softening up Spencer with punches and looks for a hcoke, but the round ends.  Spencer bleeding pretty badly.

Round 2- Natal lands a jab.  Natal switches his stance.  Natal is way bigger than Spencer.  Side kick by Natal.  Spencer coming forward, but doesn’t land.  Natal lands a jab.  Spencer lands a jab.  Natal lands a kick and a jab.  Right by Spencer lands.  Right by Spencer lands.  He’s loading up that hook.  Natal gets a takedown with 3.5 minutes left.  Spencer tries to sit up on the cage and is able to work to the feet.  Spencer lands an uppercut, but eats a knee.  Spencer is sucking wind.  Nice takedown by Natal and is in the closed guard away from the cage.  Spencer holds on as Natal keeps punching the body and tries to get separated.  Natal picks up Spencer and slams him a little to get separation, but  Spencer grabs on again.  Natal is working the body form inside the guard.  One minute left.  Natal is able to posture up and is really landing some good punches.  Spencer is trying to stand up on the cage, but Natal is on his back with thirty seconds left to work.  Natal is popping spencer and has a full back mount with twenty seconds left.  Softening him up and looks like he has a tight rear naked locked in.  Looks like Spencer’s going to tough it out as the round ends.  Wow.

Round 3- Spencer needs to put the hook on Natal as soon as possible.  Natal grabs the single, but Spencer hops out of it.  Spencer knows he needs a knockout here and rushes in.  Natal goes low and gets a takedown immediately into side control with four and a half minutes left.  Natal has a crucifix psoition.  Arm trapped between his legs and is landing undefended elbows on Spencer’s head. Like a thousand of them.  Full mount and going for a kimura, but doesn’t get it.  Now going for an arm triangle.  Spencer taps and the fight is over.

Rafael Natal wins by arm triangle submission.  Natal says that he has a great body and a beautiful girlfriend.
