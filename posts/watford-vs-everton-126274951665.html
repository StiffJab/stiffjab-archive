Watford vs Everton

August 9th, 2015 3:33pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

Watford FC looked at home in their Premier League debut on Saturday, a 2-2 draw with Everton at Goodison Park.

In truth the Hornets were unlucky to come away with a single point after scoring a pair of stylish goals and showing stout shape and defense for most of the match. Watford looked on their way to a stunning opening victory when Mexican national Miguel Layun fired home a side volley in the 26th minute, a smashing strike that stunned the opening day crowd.
