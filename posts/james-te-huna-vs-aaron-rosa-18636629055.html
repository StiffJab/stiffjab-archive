James Te Huna vs. Aaron Rosa

March 2nd, 2012 8:50pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

Round 1- Te Huna lands a leg kick.  Te Huna lands a left hand that looks like it connected.  Te Huna pushes Rosa against the cage.  Te Huna landing bodyshots against the cage, but not doing much damage.  The fighters disengage and Te Huna lands a left.  Te Huna lands another left and I think I heard his nose break.  That was disgusting.  Te Huna is landing uppercut after uppercut.  Rosa is bleeding horribly from a gash over hs left eye.  rosa is still up, but he is hurt badly.  There was another big uppercut and Rosa is out.  Te Huna has him on the ground and is landing shots.  The ref really should have stopped this by now.  Leon Roberts finally steps in.

James Te Huna by TKO at 2:08 in the first round. 

Three action packed fights with great stoppages in a row.  The Aussie fans have already gotten their money’s worth.
