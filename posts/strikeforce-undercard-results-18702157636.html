Strikeforce Undercard Results

March 3rd, 2012 9:53pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

Ryan Couture defeated Conor Heun in the third round by TKO unanswered punches to the head while Heun was facedown on the mat.

Pat Healy had an impressive arm triangle against Caros Fodor in thie third round.

Roger Bowling was having trouble with Brandon Saling until he took him to the ground, battering him from the crucifix position in the first and finishing the job in the second via strikes from the same position.

Sarah Kaufman defeated Alexis Davis in a brutal slugfest.  One of the refs scored it a draw, which was totally inexplicable given that the third round was really not that one-sided.  The other two scored it 29-28 for Kaufman who will probably get the winner of Tate-Rousey if Zuffa keeps Strikeforce on life support for long enough for there to be another women’s bantamweight title fight after tonight.
