Joey Gambino vs Steven Siler

June 22nd, 2012 7:14pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

by Dr. Octagon, J.D.

There’s lots of blood on the mat. Jeez they must have started those undercard fights early. I just got home from my real job that doesn’t pay me in Scotch. Another card on FuelTV and my poor woman has to listen to me bitching again about the low quality of this shitty regular definition feed.

Round 1: Joey Gambino kicks and Steven Siler goes for the takedown, but doesn’t get it. He pushes Gambino against the cage. Gambino turns him around and eats a knee that cuts him on the forehead, while working for the takedown. Blood is streaming down Gambino’s head. Gambino kicks, Siler catches the leg and lands two nice punches.  Siler has a standing guillotine, but lets it go and lands more knees.

Gambino lands a big left and Siler looks like his knees buckled, as he shoots for the takedown on the way down. Siler gets the takedown and is working punches from the crucifix. Gambino gives up his back and tires to stand. He is eating a shitload of punches and bleeding like a motherfucker. Gambino turns over and in the scramble, Siler sinks in a deep guillotine choke for the win.

Steven Siler by tapout via guillotine at 2:47 in the first round.
