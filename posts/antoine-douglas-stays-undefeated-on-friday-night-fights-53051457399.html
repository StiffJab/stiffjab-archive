Antoine Douglas Stays Undefeated on Friday Night Fights

June 15th, 2013 5:30pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

Antoine Douglas file photo by Trey Pollard for StiffJab.com 

by Gautham Nagesh

As we’ve noted before, whoever is managing the career of local middleweight prospect Antoine “Action” Douglas clearly knows what they are doing.

The 20-year-old has just eight professional fights, and half of them have been televised on ESPN. Douglas made his latest appearance on national TV last night, stopping veteran Ibahiem King in the final round of a scheduled four on Friday Night Fights from West Orange, N.J. King quit on his stool before the fourth round after taking a beating from the D.C. native.
