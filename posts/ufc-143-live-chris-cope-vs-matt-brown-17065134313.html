UFC 143 Live: Chris Cope vs Matt Brown

February 4th, 2012 8:52pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

by Dr. Octagon, J.D.

Both these guys hail from “The Ultimate Fighter."

Round 1-Chris Cope gets Matt Brown against the fence and he’s doing some damage. Matt Brown lands a nice knee as they disengage. Matt Brown works the takedown, but Cope stands up easily. Now Brown has Cope pinned against the cage, but Cope turns him around.

Brown spins Cope and looks for the takedown. He sweeps Cope to the ground, but Cope is able to get up. Brown lands a few more knees in the clinch. Cope lands a nice leg kick. Not much happened that round, but I think Brown wins it 10-9

Round 2-Brown lands a nice combination and it looks like Cope is bleeding. Brown lands a right to the ear and then drops Cope with a left hook. Brown lands a few more on the ground and Kim Winslow dives between them. Nice stoppage by everyone’s favorite female referee. Cope doesn’t know where he is; he tries to wrestle Kim off of him. Matt Brown by KO at 1:19 in the 2nd round.
