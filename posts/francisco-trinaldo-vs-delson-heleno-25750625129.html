Francisco Trinaldo vs. Delson Heleno

June 23rd, 2012 9:22pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

Round 1- Nice front kick by Trinaldo.  Heleno gets popped when he goes in for the shot.  He keeps coming for it, but Trinaldo stays on his feet and pops Heleno with knees to the body.  Heleno shoots and falls down and Trinaldo lands a nice punch while Heleno is on the ground.  Knees by the body by Trinaldo.  Heleno is on his knees and has Trinaldo wrapped up against the cage.  Fighters stand.  Looks like some of these knees by Heleno are low blows, but Trinaldo hasn’t complained yet.  Heleno goes for the takedown and Trinaldo lands a knee to the body.  Trinaldo landing punches to the head on Heleno who is still working for the takedown.  Trinaldo warned about 12 to 6 elbows and goes back to working the head and body of the turtled Heleno.  Heleno is on his back and Trinaldo is landing heavy hammerfists while Heleno does nothing to defend other than covering his ears.  Herb Dean stops it.

Francisco Trinaldo by TKO
