Frankie Edgar vs Jose Aldo

February 3rd, 2013 12:58am by Stiff Jab Tumblr

by Dr. Octagon, J.D.

Frankie Edgar is coming off a pair of close losses to lightweight champ Benson Henderson.

He won the second fight, as anyone but the terrible judges could see. Now Edgar’s going down a class to fight Jose Aldo, who is still bigger than him. It will be interesting watching Edgar fight someone close to his size.

Edgar’s friend JWoww is cageside. Aldo comes out to a Rihanna song. I don’t know how this sort of music gets you pumped up to kick ass, but to each his own. Aldo is a killer and has crushed everyone in his path since 2006. I’m totally psyched for this fight.Tough matchup for Frankie, as he doesn’t deal well with leg kicks, and Aldo kicks legs better than just about anyone.
