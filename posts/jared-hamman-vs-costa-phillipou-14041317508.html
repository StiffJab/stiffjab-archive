Jared Hamman vs Costa Phillipou

December 10th, 2011 8:16pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

by Dr. Octagon, J.D.

Round 1-Hamman has a dope moustachio so I expect him to win. First punch of the fight and Costa lands a left hook. Costa lands another right. Hamman shoots and gets popped twice.

Hamman is working his leg kicks and gets tagged with a right, comes back and Costa lands another big right hand. Hamman’s on brainstem and grabs a leg to survive. Philippou cranking the neck, but Hamman says he is okay. Phillipu lets him stand up and they start exchanging again.

Hamman is taking an amazing amount of punishment. He just took another punch that would have knocked out any other fighter. Really head-rattling. Phillipou lets him up one more time and finally shuts the light off with a massive left hook.

Phillipou by KO. Yowzers. These undercard fighters are not lasting very long.
