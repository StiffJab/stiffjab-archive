Cezar Ferreira vs Sergio Moraes

June 24th, 2012 12:04am by Stiff Jab Tumblr

by Dr. Octagon, J.D.

Another fucking Ultimate Fighter Brazil fight.  What a bunch of bullshit.  This Pay Per View should have been half-priced. The only fighter worth paying to watch so far has been Werdum. Let’s hope this one is better than the last amateur match we just witnessed. Live updates after the jump:[[MORE]]

Round 1: Moraes got poked in the eye. Fight resumes.  Capoira kick by Moraes lands.  Been seeing a lot of weird kicks in the last two days.  That reminds me of some shit that Ebersole threw yesterday.  Leg kicks from Moraes.  Sergio throwing punches that are no danger to anyone.  So wild.  Big kick to the body from Sergio.  Moraes lands a glancing straight left.  Moraes trying to find the right range.  Crowd booing.  These Brazilians demand action at all times.  Elbow from Moraes.  Cock shot by Ferreira.  Head kick by Ferreira who swarms and eats some shots from Moraes.  Left high kick by Ferreira is blocked.

10-9 Ferreira.

Round 2- Left by Ferreira.  Ferreira throws an uppercut.  Moraes pulls guard and Ferreira throws him off and lets him stand up.  Ferreira with a head kick.  Moraes goes down hard.  Not sure if it was from a punch or not, but Mores is bleeding from the ear.  Moraes with an enormous kick to the head and swarms Moreas with hammerfists.  Lets Moraes stand up again.  Moreas’ face is massively fucked up.  He’s bleeding horribly.  Ferreira throws another big head kick that misses.  Moraes throws another big head kick.  Ferreira throws the flying knee.  Moraes tries to wind up the crowd.  Fighters are not really engaging at all.  Moraes catches Ferreira with a left.  Moraes follows a dazed Ferreira around the cage.  What a comeback.  Ferreira is hurt now, but recovers and there is another long period of inaction before the round ends.

Round 3- Fighters hug.  I hate it when they do that.  Ferreira landed another one there and lets Moraes up, but Moraes takes his time.  Moraes looks tired.  Getting up slowly does not look good for the judges.  Long period of inaction.  Moraes landed a right.  Another long period of inaction.  Moraes does not seem aware that he is down on the scorecards, just letting time run off the clock.  Brazilians are booing.  What a terrible fight. Moraes lands a heavy right on Ferreira and then doesn’t push forward any more.  Moraes has now landed two strikes I think and he is winning the round.  Ferreira lands a hard left that puts Moraes on his ass and then kicks him in the butt as the round ends.

Tough round to score, but I give it to Moraes 10-9

I have it 29-28 for Ferreira.  Judges unanimously score it 29-28 for Ferreira.  What a terrible “co-main event.”
