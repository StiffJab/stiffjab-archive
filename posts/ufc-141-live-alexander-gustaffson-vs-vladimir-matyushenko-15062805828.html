UFC 141 Live: Alexander Gustaffson vs Vladimir Matyushenko

December 30th, 2011 10:48pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

by Dr. Octagon, J.D.

Alexander Gustaffson has a piled up wins against bums. Vladimir “the Janitor” Matyushkenko has also beaten his share of bums recently. If you want to get an idea of how old the Janitor is, he’s listed as the Soviet National Wrestling Champion but they don’t say what year. So he’s been fighting since at least 1991. Matyushenko’s advanced age means he’ll probably lose.

Round 1-The Janitor rushes in with strikes but doesn’t land anything. Vlad goes in and runs right into a jab. Gustaffson swarms as Vlad turtles up, forcing referee Yves Lavigne to end the fight.
