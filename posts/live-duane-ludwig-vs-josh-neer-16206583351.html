Live: Duane Ludwig vs Josh Neer

January 20th, 2012 10:26pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

by Dr. Octagon, J.D.

Round 1-The fighters exchange and Josh Neer pushes Duane Ludwig against the cage. Ludwig lands a knee as the fighters disengage. Ludwig is winning the standup. Neer looks slow.

Neer lost his mouthpiece, so they stop the action. There’s a cut starting to open on Neer’s eye. Neer needs to get a takedown, he’s eating a lot of punches from the man who owns the UFC record (unofficially, according to the Las Vegas Athletic Commission).

Neer catches a leg, works and gets the takedown. This is not where Duane wants to be. Neer is tough on the ground. Neer gets up and when Ludwig tries to get to his feet as well.

Neer sinks a tight guillotine. Neer tells referee Rosenthal that Ludwig is out and he stops the action. Sick submission in the transition. Neer by guillotine.
