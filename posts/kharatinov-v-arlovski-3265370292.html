Kharatinov v. Arlovski

February 12th, 2011 11:19pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

I would like to think Arlovski is going to win this one just because if he doesn’t no one will ever pay to watch him fight again.  Then again, I said the same thing before his last fight and he just sat back and fought like a lady while Bigfoot picked him apart.  Hopefully we’ll see a little more urgency here.  Arlovski has not looked good in a really really long time.  Kharitonov is the favorite here and I think he’ll probably win this one.

Round 1- Arlovski looking aggressive.  Arlovski getting the better of Kharitonov in the first few exchanges.  Kharitonov rocks Arlovski, he is hurt, hitting him against the cage, on the ground and he is out again.  Jesus, this guy needs to retire.
