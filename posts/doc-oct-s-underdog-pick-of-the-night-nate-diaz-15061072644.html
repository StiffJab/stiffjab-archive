Doc Oct's Underdog Pick of the Night: Nate Diaz

December 30th, 2011 10:13pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

by Dr. Octagon, J.D.

Donald Cerrone has looked good lately, but he has fought a ton of bums. Nate Diaz is a volume puncher with sick submissions against pretty solid competition. Still, Cerrone is definitely the harder puncher. I’m really pumped for this fight.

The reason I’m still totally down on Cerrone is his loss to Jamie Varner, who is a total bum. Currently Diaz is paying 2:1 on Bovada.lv (Bodog’s new shadier moniker). That’s a great price for Diaz, who has a solid chance of winning.

I’m not saying Diaz is a sure thing, but I think he’s got the best price on the main card.
