Lumumba Sayers Vs. Scott Smith

March 3rd, 2012 10:47pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

Round 1- Lumumba is letting the hands go.  Sayers gets the takedown and has Smith’s back.  Back to the feet and Lumumba is teeing off, picks up Smith and slams him hard.  Lumumba sinks a deep guillotine and finishes the fight as Smith taps out.

Lumumba Sayers by guillotine with 1:34 left in the first. 

Would be very surprised if Scott Smith doesn’t get cut.
