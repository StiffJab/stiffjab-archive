UFC 156 Recap: Facebook Prelims

February 2nd, 2013 8:28pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

by Dr. Octagon, J.D.

Francisco Rivera vs. Edwin Figueroa: Both guys missed weight on their first attempt, and cutting the weight appeared to take a lot out of Figueroa.  Francisco beat him up and finished him in the second round with a flurry of punches. Rivera also looked winded, but he was punching furiously for what seemed like the all of the second round.

Chico Camus vs. Dustin Kimura: Kimura dominated this fight on the ground. even though Chico brought there purposely in the first two rounds. Kimura was threatening submissions the entire time and was able to lock in the rear naked choke in the third.
