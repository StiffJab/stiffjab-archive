Rich Attonito vs Jake Hecht

December 10th, 2011 7:59pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

by Dr. Octagon, J.D.

Round 1-Attonito lands a nice right. Hecht kicks and Attonito catches it and drags Hecht to the ground into the crucifix position, but ends up in full guard. Attonito lands an elbow from inside guard. Hecht looking for submissions and landing elbows from the bottom. Attonito is landing a few more shots. Nice two punch combos, first to the body and then to the head. Hecht took a lot of damage that round. 10-9 Attonito. 

Round 2-Fighters exchange, Attonito comes out with the better punch. Hecht shoots, but Attonito stuffs it and pushes Hecht against the fence. Attonito going for the takedown on his knees and Hecht lands two elbows to the side of Attonito’s head. Attonito looks like he is out. Hecht follows him to the ground and lands another two dozen punches before Rosenthal stops it.

Hecht by TKO in round 2.
