Mismatches Mar 'Friday Night Fights'

February 19th, 2011 8:25pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

Hometown middleweight prospect Fernando Guerrero cruised to a points win against Derrick Findley (17-5, 11 KOs) on ESPN2’s “Friday Night Fights” in front of a decidedly partisan crowd in Salisbury, Md. Guerrero (21-0, 16 KOs) simply out-classed the former bodybuilder, who spent much of the later rounds covering up.

Unbeaten welterweight Shawn Porter (18-0, 13 KOs) also kept his record unblemished with a unanimous decision against crafty veteran Anges Adjaho (25-5, 14 KOs). Both young men would be better served if their next fights came against a better quality of opposition.

D.C. super middleweight prospect Dominic Wade upped his record to 11-0 (8 KOs) with a four-round clean sweep of the cards while handing Grover Young (4-1, 2 KOs) his first professional loss.
