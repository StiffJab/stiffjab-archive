UFC on Fox: Facebook Undercard Results

April 20th, 2013 5:02pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

Great card for free tonight on Fox.  I’m really excited.  Two fast ones on the undercard.

Yoel Romero landed a flying knee and followed up on the ground to defeat Clifford Starks in the first round.

Anthony Njokuani won in the second round when Roger Bowling walked straight into a left.  No punches were needed on the ground as Bowling was clearly out.

And there you have it.  The UFC has two exciting finishes to plug into its live feed in case any of these fights end early.  I’m sure you’ll see at least one of them throughout the course of the night.
