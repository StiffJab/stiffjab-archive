Jay Hieron vs Tyron Woodley

February 2nd, 2013 9:15pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

by Dr. Octagon, J.D.

Tyron Woodley stopped the much smaller Jay Hieron in just 36 seconds to win by KO at UFC 156.

I’ve got no idea how Tyron Woodley made weigh, because he looked absolutely enormous. He landed a lunging right hook, and Hieron went down hard. Woodley finished it on the ground with a few extra shots before referee Herb Dean stepped in.

It was fast, and it wasn’t pretty.
