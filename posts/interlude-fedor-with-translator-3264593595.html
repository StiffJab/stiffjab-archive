Interlude: Fedor with Translator

February 12th, 2011 10:37pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

Interview with a doughy Fedor and his doughy translator. Fedor says he’s not doing anything different. He says that he listens to Russian music without a beat because he doesn’t like modern pop music.

That was an awkward interview, taken by someone who does not appear to have any familiarity with the sport. Fedor looked annoyed. At least she had a nice face.
