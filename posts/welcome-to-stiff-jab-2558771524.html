Welcome to Stiff Jab.

January 1st, 2011 6:09pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

We hope you stick around, or at least check back once in a while.

This is a Tumblr, so if you have an account you can follow us or otherwise full posts are available via RSS feed.

We’re going to be bringing you constant coverage of the fight scene, with a special focus on the East Coast and Upper Midwest. Our focus is original reporting, photography, and intelligent coverage of MMA and the Sweet Science.

We love the big names but we’re also interested in the club fighters, the local trainers, the rising amateurs that make the sports what they are. If you think you know someone worth highlighting, be sure to send them our way.

We’re always expanding both our areas of coverage and our staff, so if you’re interested please drop me a line anytime. Thanks for stopping by, we look forward to what will hopefully be an excellent 2011 for fight fans.
