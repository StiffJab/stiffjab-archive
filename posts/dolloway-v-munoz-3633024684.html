Dolloway v. Munoz

March 3rd, 2011 10:21pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

There is something about Dolloway’s face that just pisses us off.  We hope Munoz wins this one.

Round 1- Dolloway gets the takedown and has top position, but lets Munoz up. Munoz caught him and its over.

Dolloway is saying it was premature, but he was probably going to eat a few more punches; he got clipped twice pretty bad and then Munoz dropped two unblocked hammer fists on him.

Dolloway’s eyes were rolling back in his head. Yamasaki saved him some brain damage. He’s a fucking asshole. We’re glad he got KO’d.

Joe Rogan tells it like it is.  Says it was clear he got knocked out. It was. If I was reffing CG’s next fight I’d let him get hit ten times.
