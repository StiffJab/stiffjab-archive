Ramsey Nijem vs Joe Proctor

December 8th, 2012 7:12pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

Dr. Octagon, J.D.

Round 1: The fighters start out swinging. Ramsey Nijem kicks and shoots; Joe Proctor has his neck in a guillotine and is cranking the neck. Joe loses it and now throws a knee with the head locked up. Ramsey gets his head out and the fighters exchange, Ramsey gets Joe’s head and lands a trio of knees.

Crane kick by Ramsey. Joe is hurt on the ground, Ramsey swarms and is on Joe’s back, teeing off on his head. Ramsey takes his back and pulls Proctor down. Proctor is bleeding badly from his mouth. Ramsey has a full mount, but Joe is able to work back to his feet as he grabs hold of Ramsey’s neck again. Ramsey pushes him back to the ground. Proctor is sitting up on the fence.

Ramsey gets his back as he turtles up, but Joe rolls out of it. The fighters exchange and Ramsey whiffs on a head kick. Ramsey comes forward again and Joe Proctor tags him with a pair of hooks. Ramsey is down on his back and Proctor is in half guard with 45 seconds left to work. Proctor is landing some elbows; he gets in North-South position and lands a few more shots as the round ends.

10-9 Nijem.[[MORE]]

Round 2: Joe comes right out of the gate with punches, clinches, and lands a knee. Proctor lands a right. Proctor lands a left hook. Proctor lands a right, Ramsey throws a kick. Proctor eats a right. Ramsey lands a leg kick. Right hook by Ramsey. Ramsey lands an overhand right. Body kick by Nijem. Leg kick by Ramsey. Right hook by Ramsey, countered with a right hook by Proctor.

Ramsey shoots and Proctor gets taken down, now has his guard closed. Proctor looking for an armbar as Ramsey steps out. Proctor pulls Ramsey down and looks for a front choke. Joe is trying to get it standing with Ramsey on the ground. Proctor is bleeding from his mouth all over Ramsey. Ramsey gets out, but Proctor has a good hold on his neck with a minute still left to work. Ramsey stands up, but Proctor still has the neck. Ramsey gets out of it and Joe Proctor goes for a kimura. He lets it go. The round ends with Ramsey on top.

Round 3: Ramsey with a leg kick. Ramsey with an overhand right to the head. Body kick by Ramsey. Left hand by Ramsey and Joe counters with a right of his own. Ramsey shoots and Proctor goes for an armbar to kimura. Ramsey postures up and grabs Joe’s neck; he lands shots to Proctor’s body with the other hand. Head punches by Ramsey. Joe gets to his feet, grabs the guillotine, and tries to pull guard, but he doesn’t have Nijem’s neck at all, loses it and has Ramsey on top of him in a fight that is tied at best.  That was fucking stupid.

Proctor is able to work to the top and has a really nice kimura. Scratch that, he switched to an armbar locked in, but he’s against the fence and Ramsey is able to get out of it and drop a few more punches from the top position. Proctor gets on top but is not working nearly hard enough. Ramsey gets to his feet and tees off on Proctor with some punches, a front kick, two knees, and a few more punches to end the fight. That’s how you close out a round

I have this one 29-28 for Nijem.

Judges score it 29-28 (twice) and 30-27 for Nijem.
