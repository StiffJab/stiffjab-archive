Strikeforce Heavyweight Tournament

June 18th, 2011 10:09pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

Time for the second leg of the Strikeforce Tournament. In case you don’t recall, the previous leg of the tournament saw the two fighters with the most name recognition in the tournament get easily beaten by their lesser-known opponents.

While we predicted an orderly winding down of Strikeforce even before the tournament began, it was this disastrous initial leg of the tournament that caused the powers that be at Strikeforce to concede defeat and sell out to Dana White.

Announcer Mauro Ranallo is back again. We hate this guy so much. And here is Frank Shamrock whose orthodontist should surely be fired by now. We can’t even remember the last time we saw this dumbass without braces.
