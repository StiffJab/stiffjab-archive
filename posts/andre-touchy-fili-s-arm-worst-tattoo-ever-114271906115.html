Andre “Touchy”Fili’s Arm: Worst Tattoo Ever?

March 21st, 2015 10:19pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

The bottom part of Andre Fili’s arm tattoo is clearly a vicious wolf out for blood, but the top of it looks like another hairless mutant wolf with no eyes and human teeth coming out of its head.

What the fuck is that thing? Anyway, Fili got triangled in the first round pretty much immediately. Godofredo “Pepey” Castro jumped into a beautiful triangle and stayed with it.
