Ryan Bader v. Jon Jones

February 5th, 2011 11:27pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

Round 1- Jones shoots on bader and has side control.  Now north south, chinning his stomach.  Talk about unorthodox striking.  Cranking on Bader’s neck from the top.  Bader escapes.  High kick by jones.  Bader tries to shoot and is stuffed.  Jones just jumped over Bader and hits him with elbows to the body. Great first round.

Dominant round by Jones.  10-9

Round 2- Jones with a crisp hook.  Bader is looking tentative and coming up short with his punches.  Cool jumpkick blocked by Bader.  Superman elbow by jones, but he got tagged by Bader.  Bader has his hands low right now.  Bader with a huge swing over Jones’ head.  Nice punch leg kick combination.  Bader gets the clinch, but is turned away yet again.  Jones with a big leg kick, bader treading lightly on that front foot.  Bader shoots and Jones backs up.  Kick to the body by Jones and ends up on top after Bader tries to catch his leg.  Guillotine from the top.  Jones wins. 

Sick fight.  He manhandled Bader on the ground.  Dana just said Jones gets the title shot.  Rua has got to be terrified right now.
