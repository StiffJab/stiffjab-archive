Watching Durant over the past year has convinced me he is the league’s most lethal scorer and likely heir apparent to Kobe as its premier crunch-time player.

DWade has likewise shown the ability to close in big moments, a trait that still appears to elude LeBron thanks to his still-improving long-range jump shot.
