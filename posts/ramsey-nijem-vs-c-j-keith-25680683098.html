Ramsey Nijem vs C.J. Keith

June 22nd, 2012 8:10pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

by Dr. Octagon, J.D.

Round 1: The fighters lock up and C.J. Keith lands a raw nut shot. Yowzers. Ramsey Nijem with a nice kick-punch combo. Nijem goes for a single leg and ends up on his back, with Keith on top. Keith works the mount, but Ramsey pops up to his feet and grabs another single leg. Nijem gets a takedown, but Keith is sitting up on the cage.

Ramsey keeps working and gets a takedown into a full mount. He lands a sick elbow. Ramsey raining down punishment with almost three minutes left in the round. Keith gives up his back and the ref stops the fight. It looks like it was an early stoppage. Really fucking early stoppage by  refereeYves Lavigne unless there was an oral tapout or some shit like that. Ramsey Nijem by TKO due to strikes at 2:29 in the first.
