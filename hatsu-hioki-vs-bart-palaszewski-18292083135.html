Hatsu Hioki vs. Bart Palaszewski

February 25th, 2012 10:26pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

Bart has had a ton of fights.   Last fight I saw of his, he knocked out Tyson Griffin after Griffin came in heavy. This shit is brought to you by full metal jousting.  Jeez.  talk about a ghetto advertisement.  If we’re going to bring back medieval sports, the first one should be bear baiting.  You know, something it would be entertaining to bet on.  Hatsu is a veteran from Sengoku.  Not well known, but a truly great featherweight.

Round 1- Bart caught a left and fell down, but popped up quickly.  Hioki is landing some nice jabs.  Hioki gets a leg and is working for the takedown, Bart goes for the guillotine, but Hioki gets out.  Now Hioki is in side control and has Bart’s arm.  The crowd is chanting his name.  Now he is pounding Bart with hammer fists.  Great top control.  Bart gives up his back and Hioki has his arm.  He’s got a perfect armbar across the face, but Bart gets out of it.  Hioki has an omoplata, but Bart rolls out of that as well.  Hioki is now landing elbows from the top.  Hatsu looks dope so far.  He’s able to posture up and is popping Bart.

Round 2- Bart lands a nice right. Hioki lands a head kick.  Hioki lands a jab.  Bart throws a combination.  Bart lands a right.  Hioki goes for a high kick and Bart ducks under it.  Both fighters throw leg kicks.  Bart is landing nice leg kicks.  Bart is throwing nice combinations.  Hioki kicks Bart in Bart’s penis.  Bart gets him back.  Even exchange on the groin kicks. Bart landed a nice jab right there.  Hioki shoots for the takedown and gets Bart’s back as the round ends.

Round 3- Bart knows he needs to win this round  so he comes out swinging.  Hioki has Bart against the cage and is able to trip him and get him to the ground.  Going for the arm triangle, but Bart is defending nicely.  Hioki is landing some hammerfists.  Hioki trying to pass guard and is able to get into sideguard.  Has a mounted crucifix and is landing elbows and a lot of punches.  Bart tries to spin and Hioki gets his back.  Hioki has the body triangle tight and is going for the rear naked,  but Bart is holding on to his hands.  Nice elbow by Hioki, who is doing a lot of damage.  Hatsu pulls Bart to the ground and is landing elbows.  He’s got the rear naked, but it’s up too high.  Hioki lands a trio of elbows to the ear as the round ends.

29-28 Hioki.  Hioki looked great.  Judges score it 29-28x2 and 30-27 for Hioki, who is probably going to get a title shot from this one.
