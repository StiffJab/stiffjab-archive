Gabriel Gonzaga vs. Ednaldo Oliveira

January 14th, 2012 9:04pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

by Dr. Octagon, J.D.

Gonzaga is reported to have slick BJJ skills, but always ends up in striking battles. Oliveira looks soft. Sort of skinny and fat at the same time.

Round 1-Gonzaga gets the single and scores a takedown. Oliveira gets up, but Gonzaga puts him back down. Oliveira is trying to sit up on the cage, but Gonzaga gets his back and chokes him out.

Gonzaga with the win by submission.
