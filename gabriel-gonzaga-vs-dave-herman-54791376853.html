Gabriel Gonzaga vs Dave Herman

July 6th, 2013 9:00pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

by Dr. Octagon, J.D.

Gabriel Gonzaga scored a lightning-fast knockout over Dave Herman in this heavyweight bout.

Herman threw two push kicks and landed a leg kick, but Gonzaga countered with an overhand right that floored Herman. Referee Kim Winslow seemed to be a mile away when the first punch landed, and finally attempted to stop the 260-pound Brazilian from raining down punches on the unconscious Herman after a considerable pause.

The official result was Gonzaga by knockout in 17 seconds.
