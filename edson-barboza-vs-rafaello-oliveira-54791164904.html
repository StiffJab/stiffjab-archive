Edson Barboza vs Rafaello Oliveira

July 6th, 2013 8:57pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

by Dr. Octagon, J.D.

This fight ended in the second round due to leg kicks. Edson Barboza really knows how to rip them. He just attacked Rafaello Oliveira’s front leg the whole fight. Oliveira’s knee was swollen and bloody, and by the second round he could barely stand. Referee Herb Dean waved it off after Oliveira took a few more heavy leg kicks.
