July 31st, 2011 12:36am by Stiff Jab Tumblr

Jesus, that lady fight was unbearable. I’m totally hammered, so I’ll write up all the shit undercard fights tomorrow. Maybe. They were fucking awful. 

Can’t wait for Strikeforce to get absorbed by UFC so I don’t have to waste my time watching this bullshit. Lots of people are tuning in for Fedor vs Dan Henderson, that undercard had no business on Pay Per View. On to the main event:

Round 1-Fedor goes right at it but it looks like Henderson got him. Henderson with the superman punch. This is a fucking slugfest already. Jesus. Henderson ties up Fedor against the cage. Fedor looks like he has a bit of swelling over the right eye. 

Henderson is working leg kicks from the clinch. Fedor should be able to push the smaller man off, but he isn’t doing anything. Fedor just getting hit with leg kicks. Still not really doing anything. Use some Sambo bra.

Henderson looks totally gassed. The lardass Russian starts landing some shots and killing Henderson from the top. Henderson lands a shot. Fedor is turned over and Herb Dean ends it. Henderson wins. 

I told you to bet on him so if you didn’t it’s your own goddamn fault. On the replay it looks like Henderson caught Fedor in the scramble. Totally thought Fedor was winning that one.

Herb Dean ended it a little quick and I’m sure Fedor is going to bitch about it. I actually think he was still conscious, but that should have been allowed to go for a few more shots. Gotta let these guys fight.

Henderson wins, but I think Fedor still had his wits about him. Hot tip of the day from Stiff Jab people. Hope you made some cash on this one.
