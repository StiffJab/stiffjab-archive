Mitch Clarke vs John Cholish

December 10th, 2011 8:32pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

by Dr. Octagon, J.D.

Round 1-Cholish looks like a mini Bisping. Cholish lands a few punches, shoots but shot is stuffed. Clarke goes for standing choke but doesn’t get it. Scramble, Mitch pulls Cholish down, but he keeps popping up. Cholish rolls into a knee bar but lets it go and is now in on top in Clarke’s guard. Cholish postures up and lands a nice short elbow. Cholish lets Mitch up. Fighters exchange a few times with no really meaningful strikes being landed, but Cholish probably got the better of Clarke.

Round 2-Cholish is looking sharp. Landing leg kicks and a few punches. Fighters clinch and it looks like Cholish lands a knee. Canadian Clarke is looking winded.  Cholish lands a sharp right hand. Clarke’s nosepiece looks a little fucked. Nice right hook by Cholish. Cholish lands a leg kick and then backs away. 

Cholish is teeing off with his right on Clark. Clarke needs to start putting his left hand up. Cholish gets a double leg takedown and falls into full guard. Cholish lands a nice elbow from the top. Mitch is sucking wind. Clarke rolls out there is a scramble and Cholish gets Clarke’s back.

Clark is bleeding really badly from his face, which is going to stain the mat badly over by the Xyience ad. Clark takes massive amounts of punishment and Cholish wins  via TKO.
