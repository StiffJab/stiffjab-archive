Stiff Jab Presents: The Fourth Judge Podcast Episode 37

October 19th, 2014 8:32pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

Things are a little slow for the Knicks this week, so the boys bring in a guest to spice things up, kinda like how strong marriages built on trust bring in a third lover from time to time.

This week’s guest is former proprietor of Walkoff Walk, Rob Iracane, who explains to us that no, we’re not crazy, there really is no reason to watch the World Series.

Also discussed: Doritos, Frasier, pilates, and judging books by their covers.
