Amir Sadollah vs. DaMarques Johnson

March 26th, 2011 11:03pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

We’ve got Amir in this one. We’ve loved him ever since he beat C.B. Dolloway’s cocky ass twice on TUF. It could go either way. The Darkness is pretty good.

Round 1- Wild striking, Amir slips and is on the ground. Amir pops up easily and it is back to the feet where he has an advantage.

Just as Joe Rogan says Amir’s Muy Thai is technical, Amir lands a precise strike to the Darkness’ cock. DaMarques catches a kick and has Amir on the ground again.  Darkness lets Amir up and they are back to boxing again. 

DaMarques looks a lot sharper than expected on the feet. Nice judo throw by the Darkness. Amir pops back up and gets a takedown of his own, but is in closed guard and isn’t doing any damage. Damarques goes for a triangle and Amir is able to posture up and get a punch in before the round ends.

DaMarques took that one for sure 10-9

Round 2-Damarques is eating knees from the clinch. Looks hurt. Damarques comes back and scores a few shots of his own. Vicious knees to the body as DaMarques goes down. Now landing elbows to the head.

This might not last much longer. He is standing up and teeing off. DaMarques is still defending himself, but he is hurt badly. More knees to the body. Those look really painful. The Darkness manages to get up.

Nice trip by Amir and he has a full mount. Has his arm caught and is pounding the face. This is a really bad position. Mario Yamasaki finally puts an end to it.

Amir by TKO. Nice win against a quality TUF alum.

Amir is classy as always and gives The Darkness props for taking the fight on such short notice.
