Donald Cerrone v. Paul Kelly

February 5th, 2011 9:47pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

Round 1-Kelly goes for the head punch when Cerrone goes to touch gloves.  The Liverpudlian is fighting dirty.  Cerrone immediately takes him down.  The Brit is working the knees.  Cerrone working the leg kicks.  Cerrone’s eye is looking bad.  Kelly winning it on the feet with crisp punches.  Cerrone gets top control, but Kelly gets a hold of his arm and he doesn’t do much damage initially, but manages to work some elbows at the end of the round and realy opens his eye up.  Blood is flowing and Cerrone helps him up.

Cerrone stole that round at the end 10-9

Round 2-Cerrone taking some shots, but his leg kicks are still crisp.  Now Kelly with some leg kicks.  Cerrone with the takedown and gets the half guard, looks like Kelly’s eye is open again.  Tries for the arm triangle and Kelly gets out of it but ends up in the full mount.  Cerrone transfers to the back with lots of time left.  Lots of punches from behind and sinks the rear naked for the victory. Karma is a bitch son.

USA! USA! USA! Cowboy puts on his stupid Tapout cowboy hat.  Kelly tries to explain his dirty tricks and we have no idea what the fuck he said, there is no way that is the Queen’s English.
