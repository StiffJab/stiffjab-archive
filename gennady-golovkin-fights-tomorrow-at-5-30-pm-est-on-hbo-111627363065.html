Gennady Golovkin Fights Tomorrow at 5:30 PM EST on HBO

February 20th, 2015 11:37pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

“Gennady Golovkin” by Zaqir Ismile ft. Владимир Шаповалов - http://yvision.kz/post/361606. Licensed under Copyrighted free use via Wikimedia Commons - http://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Gennady_Golovkin.jpg#mediaviewer/File:Gennady_Golovkin.jpg

That photograph above is a picture of the baddest middleweight on the planet.  When people tell me that they find boxing boring (people actually say this), I tell them to watch Gennady Golovkin fight.  He’s not super famous in the USA just yet, but he really should be.  The Kazakh middleweight has finished his last 16 fights by KO or TKO, which is amazing for a middleweight.  Fighting on HBO at 5:30 PM is probably not going to draw a ton of American eyeballs, but if you’re into boxing at all, you should watch this fight. Just make sure that you don’t turn it on late because it might not be on for very long.
