Chad Griggs vs Valentijn Overeem

June 18th, 2011 10:17pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

We have Griggs in this one. Valentijn fucking sucks. Shamrock makes a big deal of Valentijn’s 3" reach advantage. That’s not much of an advantage, but at least Frank is trying to say something intelligent.

Round 1- Valentijn throws a high kick that glances off of Griggs’ head and Griggs uses the opportunity to mash Valentijn into the cage before throwing him down to the ground.

Griggs is stuck in Valentijn’s guard and Valentijn looks to have a grip on Griggs’ arm while Grigg’s punches him in the head. Valentijn lets go of the arm and Greggs postures up and smashes Valentijn. Valentijn turns over and is getting pummeled while he turtles.

Fight is over. That was fucking fast.

On the replay, Valentijn is seen tapping to strikes. Total bitch move.
