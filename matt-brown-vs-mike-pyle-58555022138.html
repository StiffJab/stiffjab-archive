Matt Brown vs Mike Pyle

August 17th, 2013 9:20pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

by Dr. Octagon, J.D.

Sorry folks, missed a ton of fights due to predictable DVR issues, but I made it home in time for this one.

Mike Pyle is protesting the knockout. Wait until he sees the replay.
