Cheick Kongo vs Mark Hunt

February 26th, 2012 12:09am by Stiff Jab Tumblr

by Dr. Octagon, J.D.

Mark Hunt throws a leg kick and slips. Cheick Kongo pushes him against the cage, but Hunt turns Kongo around. Kongo lands a leg kick.

Hunt lands a big left on Kongo as he comes in. Kongo is hurt. Hunt lands a huge right and follows Kongo, lands two shots on the feet and a few on the ground. Referee Herb Dean stops the fight.

Mark Hunt by KO. Nice quick knockout.
