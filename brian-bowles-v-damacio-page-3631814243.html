Brian Bowles v. Damacio Page

March 3rd, 2011 9:19pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

Former WEC bantamweight champion and owner of a sick helmet haircut, Brian Bowles is in the first fight. Bowles should be considered a heavy favorite against Damacio Page, who he already guillotined once in the WEC.

Round 1-Damacio comes out swinging with a kick and a flying knee. Bowles previously said he wasn’t going to brawl with page, but it looks like that isn’t actually the plan.

Page is landing leg kicks and throwing looping hooks. Bowles lands the uppercut.  Page with the knee from the thai clinch. Bowles lands a jab. Bowles gets his eye poked and the stupid ref doesn’t stop it even after he grabs his eye.

Page throwing wildly and ends up on the ground. Bowles raining down shots from the top, sinks the guillotine, deep as hell, doesn’t tap and he’s out. That was fast. Nice choke.
