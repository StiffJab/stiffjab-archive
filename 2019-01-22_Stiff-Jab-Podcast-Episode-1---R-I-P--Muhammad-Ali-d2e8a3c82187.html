Stiff Jab Podcast Episode 1 — R.I.P. Muhammad Ali

The first episode of our podcast produced in June 2016 was recorded immediately following the death of heavyweight legend Muhammad Ali. Guest host James Marceda interviews Stiff Jab editor Gautham Nagesh about what made Ali so special both inside and outside of the ring.
