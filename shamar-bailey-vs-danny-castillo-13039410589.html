Shamar Bailey vs Danny Castillo

November 19th, 2011 8:47pm by Stiff Jab Tumblr

by Dr. Octagon, J.D.

Round 1-Shamar Bailey is fighting two pounds overweight tonight and looks it. He’s giving up a chunk of his purse to Danny Castillo. 

Castillo kicks and slips. Shamar rushes in and Castillo catches him and slams him down with a massive takedown. Shamar lands some elbows and then stands up. Castillo picks up Shamar again and slams him down. Shamar gets up. Castillo slams him down again.

Castillo is landing some nice shots. Shamar is in trouble here. Castillo passes guard with thirty seconds left. Castillo is lighting him up and the ref dives in even though there are six seconds left in the round. Shamar’s not going to be happy about that one and will surely claim that he wasn’t out.

Castllo says he felt disrespected by Bailey when he showed up overweight.
